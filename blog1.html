Phoenix and Astro developing commercial space station, Olympus, to house over 100 astronauts at once

  • By Pryce Markson
  • October 25, 2020
  • Events

Phoenix and fellow commercial space agency, Astro, recently announced their collaboration to build a commercial space station, Olympus. This new space station will be made in low Earth orbit and will allow a new era of human space exploration like never before.

Designed to open many new markets in space, Olympus will offer various services, including the research, international, and commercial sectors. The station can house up to 100 onboard crew members, who will be provided the necessary space transportation, logistics, habitation, equipment and operations, materials, and services. Phoenix and Astro have been working tirelessly to ensure that the estimated start date of operations stays within the next three years.

Summary of Olympus

Olympus features a human-centered space station with countless services and amenities offered to all. The unique reusable transports and rockets are environmentally friendly while being top-notch in design, automation, logistics, thus minimizing costs for everyone. Phoenix looks forward to continuing its work with Astro and changing the world for the better!

"Olympus represents the gateway into habitable, more importantly, sustainable space life."

- Silvina Owens, Executive Engineer
