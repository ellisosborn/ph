Phoenix Experiences

Phoenix Private Space Flights

In light of recent events, Phoenix is offering limited private space flights to parties that booked at least six months ahead of time. Private parties can view mission launches in real time and experience what it’s like to go to space. Reservations are open on a limited basis, with two flight opportunities offered per month. The flights serve as a gateway for dreamers, questioners, and lovers of the unknown to come together and experience the final frontier. Enjoy our new technology as we explore the boundaries of humanity's technological advancements.

While showing off new technologies, our private flights offer a safe and interactive environment for all who participate. As Phoenix continues to advance, we will continue to expand the length of our flights. As a team, it’s always the goal to simulate and test our journeys countless times to ensure that our customers receive the best possible experience. Be a part of a new era of humanity as we explore the outer reaches of our solar system. Take the leap of faith, and reserve a flight with Phoenix! We expect full capacity for our private space flights to return by the end of 2022.
