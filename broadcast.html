About Launch Broadcasts

Phoenix’s virtual launch broadcast utilizes online video conferencing to connect viewers from across the globe during launch. Phoenix allows the viewers to connect and discuss the program, which creates an interactive and enjoyable way to watch a rocket launch. Further, each viewer has invitation capabilities to send up to 16 friends and family additional viewing links to enjoy alongside the experience together.

Phoenix’s launch broadcast not only connects viewers but offers many other experiences as well. Phoenix presents keynote speakers, such as engine developers and launch controllers to give detailed speeches to the participants. These broadcasts provide additional behind the scenes insight into the launch, including questions and answers with astronauts. In addition, Phoenix sends all previous viewers a customized shirt and sweatshirt from our merchandise store.
