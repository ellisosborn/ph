Why Phoenix Space Exploration

Here at Phoenix, we aim to modernize unique experiences that allow ordinary civilians to take part in truly "Out of this World" adventures from the comfort of their own homes. Offering both virtual and in-person opportunities, Phoenix has something for everyone.

01

Reduced COVID-19 Exposure

Phoenix virtual events reduce the exposure of COVID-19 by allowing users to experience rocket launches from any location.

02

Escalated Interactivity

Users can utilize sensory feedback and 360° viewing through compatible virtual reality headsets on all virtual experiences.

03

Budget-Friendly

Phoenix offers a wide range of economical solutions compared to traditionally high-cost virtual experiences.

04

International Reach

Citizens from anywhere around the world are welcome to engage as customers and apply for remote jobs at Phoenix.

05

Limitless Customization

Phoenix offers a unique, custom-tailored experience for each and every person based on their individual preferences and budget.

06

Cross-Platform Interactions

Phoenix experiences are available on all devices, and our mobile application is available on the Android and Apple app stores.
