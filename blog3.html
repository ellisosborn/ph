Celebrity Oscar Grammer and lead project engineer Olivia Dankins to fly on Phoenix on October

  • By Jake Moss
  • September 19, 2021
  • Events

Today, Phoenix announced that actor Oscar Grammer and Olivia Dankins, Phoenix's Vice President of Mission and Flight Operations, will board Zeus.

Oscar Grammer's career as an actor, director, producer, writer, recording artist, and jockey span 30 years. Grammer is currently the host and executive producer of various science fiction fantasy shows and podcasts. His most popular show, Mysteries Uncovered, is a one-hour non-fiction series that explores the world's most fascinating, bizarre, and unexplained mysteries.

Olivia Dankins joined the Phoenix team in 2011 to oversee all Zeus flight operations, vehicle maintenance, launch, landing, and ground support infrastructure. Working with the Zeus Safety and Mission Guarantee Team, Testing and Flight Operations, Chief Engineers, and external partners, she has played a leading role in the multi-year process for certifying Zeus human flight. Before leading the mission and flight operations team at Phoenix, she was Deputy Legal Advisor and Vice President of Legal and Compliance at Phoenix, overseeing various legal, regulatory, and policy issues. She has been pivotal to the success and development here at Phoenix.

"I am humbled in flying on behalf of Team Phoenix and am excited to continue writing the history of Phoenix's crewed spaceflight," Olivia Dankins said. "I was part of an amazing effort collected for the Human Flight Certification Review (HFCR) of Zeus, a long-awaited goal that was completed in July 2021. As an engineer, lawyer, and aerospace engineer with over 20 years of experience in the aerospace industry, her place on this spot has been cemented with the amount of work and dedication she has put into this team. “I am extremely confident in our family here at Phoenix, and I feel extremely fortunate to have the minds here supporting me.”
